# About Tunghai University Global MBA

## Our Mission

The Tunghai University Global MBA program is designed to develop principled business leaders who can navigate the complexities of global markets while maintaining ethical standards and cultural sensitivity. We combine rigorous academic training with practical experience to prepare our graduates for leadership roles in the dynamic Asian business environment.

## Our Vision

To be the premier MBA program in Taiwan for international students and professionals seeking to build careers in Asia's growing economies.

## Program History

Established in [Year], the Tunghai Global MBA has grown from a small cohort of local students to a diverse international program attracting talented professionals from across Asia and beyond. Our program is accredited by AACSB International, placing us among the top 5% of business schools worldwide.
