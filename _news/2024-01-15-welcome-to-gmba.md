We are excited to announce the arrival of our new Global MBA cohort for the 2024 academic year! This year, we have welcomed 45 talented professionals from 12 different countries, bringing diverse perspectives and experiences to our program.

## A Diverse and Dynamic Cohort

Our new students come from a wide range of industries, including technology, finance, manufacturing, consulting, and healthcare. With an average of 5.2 years of professional experience, they bring valuable real-world insights to classroom discussions and group projects.

### Class Profile Highlights:

- **45 students** from **12 countries**
- **65% international students** representing Asian and global markets
- **Average work experience**: 5.2 years
- **Industry diversity**: Technology (25%), Finance (20%), Manufacturing (18%), Consulting (15%), Healthcare (12%), and Others (10%)

## Orientation Week

The orientation week was packed with activities designed to help students get to know each other and familiarize themselves with the program:

- Campus tour and facility introductions
- Team-building activities
- Introduction to faculty and staff
- Academic program overview
- Career services workshop
- Cultural exchange dinner

## Looking Ahead

As we embark on this exciting journey together, we look forward to:

- Engaging case discussions and collaborative projects
- Company visits to leading Taiwanese and international firms
- Guest lectures from industry leaders
- International exchange opportunities
- Career development workshops

We wish all our new students the very best as they begin their Global MBA journey at Tunghai University!
