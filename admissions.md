# Admissions

## Application Requirements

### Academic Background
- Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution
- Minimum GPA of 3.0 (or equivalent)
- All majors welcome to apply

### Work Experience
- Minimum 2 years of full-time professional work experience preferred
- Fresh graduates with exceptional qualifications may be considered

### English Proficiency
One of the following test scores (not older than 2 years):
- TOEFL iBT: 80 or above
- IELTS: 6.5 or above
- TOEIC: 750 or above
- Native English speakers or graduates from English-taught programs may be exempt

### GMAT/GRE (Optional)
- GMAT or GRE scores are optional but recommended
- Strong scores can strengthen your application

## Application Materials

Please submit the following documents:

1. **Online Application Form**
2. **Personal Statement** (500-1000 words)
   - Your career goals and how the GMBA program fits into your plans
   - What unique perspective you bring to the program
3. **Resume/CV**
   - Education and work experience
   - Achievements and leadership roles
4. **Official Transcripts**
   - From all universities attended
   - English translation required if original is not in English
5. **Two Letters of Recommendation**
   - Preferably from professional supervisors or academic advisors
6. **English Proficiency Test Score**
7. **Copy of Passport**
8. **Application Fee Payment Receipt**

## Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer various scholarships to outstanding applicants:

### Merit-Based Scholarships
- **Presidential Scholarship**: Full tuition + stipend
- **Dean's Scholarship**: 50% tuition waiver
- **Excellence Scholarship**: 25% tuition waiver

### Need-Based Financial Aid
- Available for students demonstrating financial need
- Application required with supporting documentation

### International Student Scholarships
- Taiwan Ministry of Education Scholarship
- Tunghai University International Student Grants

### Graduate Assistantships
- Teaching or research assistantships available
- Provides tuition waiver + monthly stipend
- 10-15 hours per week commitment

## Visa Information

### Student Visa (for International Students)
After receiving your admission letter:
1. Apply for a student visa at the nearest Taiwan representative office
2. Required documents include admission letter, financial proof, and health certificate
3. Processing time: typically 5-10 business days

### Resident Permit
Upon arrival in Taiwan:
1. Apply for an Alien Resident Certificate (ARC) within 15 days
2. The university will assist with the application process

## FAQs

**Q: Can I apply if my bachelor's degree is not in business?**
A: Yes! We welcome students from all academic backgrounds.

**Q: Is work experience required?**
A: While 2 years of work experience is preferred, exceptional fresh graduates may be considered.

**Q: Do I need to speak Mandarin Chinese?**
A: No. All courses are taught in English. However, we offer optional Mandarin classes.

**Q: Can I work part-time while studying?**
A: International students can work up to 20 hours per week with proper permits.

**Q: Are internships guaranteed?**
A: While we cannot guarantee internships, we provide extensive support and connections to help students secure opportunities.
