# Program Overview

The Tunghai Global MBA is a full-time, two-year program taught entirely in English, designed for professionals who want to advance their careers in international business.

## Curriculum Structure

### Year 1: Core Foundation

**Fall Semester**
- Managerial Economics
- Financial Accounting
- Marketing Management
- Organizational Behavior
- Business Statistics
- Mandarin Chinese (Optional)

**Spring Semester**
- Corporate Finance
- Operations Management
- Strategic Management
- Managerial Accounting
- Business Analytics
- International Business

### Year 2: Specialization & Integration

**Fall Semester**
- Elective Courses (Choose 4-5)
- Capstone Project Preparation
- Industry Practicum

**Spring Semester**
- Elective Courses (Choose 3-4)
- Capstone Project
- Career Development Seminar

## Specialization Tracks

### International Business
Focus on cross-border operations, global supply chains, and emerging markets.

### Finance & Investment
Deep dive into corporate finance, investment analysis, and fintech innovations.

### Technology & Innovation Management
Explore digital transformation, innovation strategy, and entrepreneurship.

### Marketing & Brand Management
Master brand strategy, digital marketing, and consumer behavior in Asian markets.

## Learning Outcomes

Upon graduation, our students will be able to:

1. **Strategic Thinking**: Analyze complex business situations and develop effective strategies
2. **Global Perspective**: Understand and navigate international business environments
3. **Leadership Skills**: Lead diverse teams and drive organizational change
4. **Analytical Capabilities**: Use data and analytics to inform business decisions
5. **Ethical Judgment**: Make decisions that balance profitability with social responsibility
6. **Communication**: Effectively communicate across cultures and stakeholder groups
