## Visit Our Campus

### Campus Tours
We offer campus tours for prospective students and their families. Tours include:
- Visit to the College of Management facilities
- Tour of campus amenities and student life areas
- Q&A session with current students or faculty
- Information session about the program

**To schedule a tour**: Please email us at least one week in advance with your preferred date and time.

### Information Sessions
We regularly host online and in-person information sessions. Check our [News](/news) page or contact us for upcoming session dates.

### How to Get Here

**From Taichung Train Station**:
- Take Bus 300, 301, 302, or 303 to Tunghai University (約30分鐘)

**From Taichung High Speed Rail Station**:
- Take Bus 151 or 153 to Tunghai University (約45分鐘)

**From Taichung International Airport**:
- Taxi to campus (約30分鐘)

**Parking**: Visitor parking is available on campus
